Curiosity rarely arrives in a straight line. It circles things. It makes a little mess. It opens tabs, draws arrows, forgets why it came into the room, then finds the point two days later.

## A gentler system

I am trying to keep systems that allow wandering without losing everything.

- one place for rough thoughts
- one place for finished-ish notes
- one place for links that might become something

The point is not perfect order. The point is being able to return.
