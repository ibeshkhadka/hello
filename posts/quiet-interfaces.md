A quiet interface does not mean an empty one. It means the important parts know how to stand still.

## Notes to self

- Use motion as a small acknowledgement, not a drum roll.
- Let typography carry more of the mood.
- Remove explanations once the object itself makes sense.
- Leave some corners undecided.

The best personal websites feel like they were arranged for a friend who already knows how to look around.
