I keep coming back to tools that do one thing in a way that feels almost boring. A feed reader. A scratchpad. A folder of links. They do not ask for attention with fireworks.

## The useful kind of small

Small tools are not small because they lack ambition. They are small because they respect the edges of a life. They leave room around the thing you came to do.

I want more software that feels like a desk drawer: specific, personal, a little dusty, and exactly where you left it.
